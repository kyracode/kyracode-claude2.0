Horror Film · 2025

MOTHER

Dir. Kyra Jolly and Vladislav Protasov  ·  June 2025  ·  4 min

In the wake of her child's death, Gertrude Margeret Mildred Mary Crawford is overwhelmed with grief. A brief dream kicks off her spiral into insanity and soon, blood stains her hands. She resorts to rituals and the guidance of the devil's hand in the hopes of bringing back her child.

I made this film with 3 other students as the final project of our ninth grade media course. The task was to create a short horror film. I co-directed this project with another classmate.​​
